Flight Tracker

Created by Colin Bernard

Flight data is refreshed every 5 seconds. Data is provided by a co-op of ADS-B/Mode S/MLAT feeders. As this data is community driven, not all aircraft may be reported. All reported flights within 500km of CYLW (Kelowna Airport) are shown. Click an aircraft for more information.

This is a work in progress

Data provided by ADS-B Exchange
